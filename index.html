ABOUT ME

I'm a British developer and graphic designer. I'm fluent in Electron, React, TailwindCSS, plain web design, NodeJS, Java and more.
When I was about 10, I launched some app and then wondered how someone made it. Then I thought, "Hey, why don't I try make one myself," not realising what I had just got myself into. I made a really bad app in Visual Studio's visual app maker thingy. I once thought making it in some non-visual way would suck, until I tried it and loved it.
Four years later, I'm still making stuff for fun. I make websites, apps and games all the time. My by far biggest project is Withered Launcher, which is like Steam but with offline launch support, only a 5% or 10% cut of profits for paid games, and without the $100/year fee that Steam charges you to publish your game there.
